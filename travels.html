Travels

This is from this past May I took a trip to Lake Tahoe, Nevada. Lake Tahoe is one of the most beautiful places I have ever been to. While in Lake Tahoe, I got to kayak, swim, hike, and bike.

Last January I took a weekend trip to Chicago with my friends. We took the train from Ann Arbor which was very convenient. While in Chicago I saw the bean, a broadway show, and ate deep dish pizza. Chicago is a great city and I hope to go back one day.

During the Summer of 2018 I lived in Tel Aviv, Israel for six weeks. I participated in a program called the Israel Summer Business Academy. During the week we would attend class and visit companies. On the weekend, we would travel around the country of Israel.

This trip was filled with relaxation as it was exactly a week before I headed back to Ann Arbor for school. During the trip we laid on the beach, got massages, did yoga, and ate yummy meals. I hope to do a beach trip like this one next summer!

In May I traveled to Croatia through the Ross Global Imersion program. After the program ended, I extended my travels and visited Dubrovnik. Dubrovnik is a beautiful city, and many episodes of Game of Thrones was filmed here. I loved the city of Dubrovnik.

This is a picture of the cherry blossoms in Washington D.C. My older sister Caroline lived in Washington D.C. for 2 years. I loved visiting her during cherry blossom season. These trees are majestical, and I enjoyed looking at them when I visited her.
